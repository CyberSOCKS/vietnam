Accommodation

UTPHAX 2016

Participants shall arrange their own accommodations and transportation.
Please find the list of available nearby accommodations below :

  • Student Residential Halls in UUM campus (subject to availability)
  • Hotels and Homestay in Sintok and Changlun (10-15 minutes drive to UUM)

In-campus student residential halls are only provided to participating students at a rate of RM30/night/person. Once accommodation is requested, you will be charged until the night of 23rd October, regardless of when you decide to check-out of the hostel. For teams that have booked accommodation, please proceed to Dewan Muadzam Shah for room keys collection as soon as you arrive in UUM. If you have further question, please contact our Registration Unit.
